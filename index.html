Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is air duct cleaning necessary?

Yes, especially if you have visible dust, poor airflow, allergies, pets, or recent renovations.

2. How often should air ducts be cleaned?

Most homes benefit every 3–5 years, while commercial systems may need more frequent service.

3. How long does professional air duct cleaning take?

Typically 2–4 hours for residential homes, depending on system size and condition.

4. Does air duct cleaning improve HVAC efficiency?

Clean ducts allow better airflow, which can reduce strain on your HVAC system.

5. Is DIY air duct cleaning effective?

DIY methods rarely reach deep into the duct system and can cause damage without proper equipment.

6. Do you offer air duct cleaning nationwide?

Yes, Ron's Airduct Cleaning & HVAC provides air duct and HVAC cleaning services across the United States.
